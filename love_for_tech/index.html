Differences between Paddings, Margins and Border

Padding is the space between the contents and the border. For instance, if i draw box and I write some texts in the box, the little space between my texts and the line of my box is called the padding.

Margin is the space around the border. For instance, if i draw box and I write some texts in the box, the space outside my box is called the margin.

Border is the line around my box
